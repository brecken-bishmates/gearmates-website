FAQ

Got questions?

GearMates is a mobile app that helps you manage your travel and outdoor gear. It combines a personal gear inventory (called your Gear Locker), per-trip packing lists with collaborative sharing, and an AI Concierge that sends you smart, proactive suggestions — before your trip, during it, and when you get back.

GearMates is launching on iOS first. Android is on the roadmap but not confirmed with a timeline yet. Join the waitlist and you'll be first to know the moment it goes live.

We haven't announced pricing yet, but early waitlist members will get a meaningful advantage — we're committed to rewarding the people who believe in the app before it's out. Sign up and you'll hear the details first.

Not a chatbot. The AI Concierge works silently in the background and surfaces suggestion cards at the right moment. Pre-trip, it audits your gear and flags anything that should be fixed or replaced. Mid-trip, it uses your location to recommend nearby gear shops or repair spots. Post-trip, it prompts you to log condition updates on the gear you used — keeping your locker current without the hassle of doing it manually.

Yes. Your Gear Locker is completely private — only you can see what you own. On shared trips, collaborators see a combined packing list but do not see your full inventory or your private notes. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Anything you travel or adventure with — hiking gear, camera equipment, travel clothing, footwear, water filters, bags, sleeping systems, documents, electronics, and more. GearMates is designed for the full spectrum: weekend cabin trips to months-long backpacking routes.

Create a trip, invite your travel companions, and build the packing list together. Each item shows who owns it (pulled from their Gear Locker) and its packing intent — whether someone already has it, needs to buy it, wants it, or is still deciding. This eliminates the coordination mess of "wait, are you bringing the tent or am I?" Your private gear notes and inventory remain invisible to others.
